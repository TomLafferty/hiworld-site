## 📱 Our Products

### Card Valuer
An iOS app for Pokemon TCG dealers and collectors. Scan cards with your phone camera, get instant valuations using live market data. Perfect for trade shows, card shops, or sorting bulk inventory at home.

**Features:**
- Real-time camera scanning with on-device OCR
- Live market pricing from TCGPlayer & eBay
- Support for PSA, CGC, BGS, and SGC graded slabs
- 7-day price trends per card
- Offline OCR (no internet required)
- Session management with CSV export

**Status:** 🚀 Launching on the App Store

### Fantasy Sports Hub
A dedicated platform for fantasy sports enthusiasts with multi-league management, player analytics, and data-driven lineup tools.

**Features:**
- Multi-league dashboard
- Player performance analytics
- Real-time scoring
- Trade analyzer & waiver wire tools
- Cross-sport support (NFL, NBA, MLB)

**Status:** 🛠️ In Development
