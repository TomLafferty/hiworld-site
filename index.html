Card Valuer
iOS App  ·  Pokemon TCG

Instantly scan and price Pokemon Trading Card Game cards and graded slabs using your phone camera. Built for dealers, collectors, and anyone sorting bulk inventory at speed — at trade shows, card shops, or at home.

  • Real-time camera scanning with on-device OCR
  • Live market pricing from TCGPlayer & eBay data
  • PSA, CGC, BGS, and SGC graded slab support
  • 7-day price trend indicators per card
  • Session management with CSV export
  • Scanning works offline — no internet required for OCR
  • Collection history and aggregate stats

About HiWorld

Who We Are

HiWorld LLC is a US-based mobile software company focused on building apps for collectors, hobbyists, and sports enthusiasts. We believe the best tools are fast, focused, and purpose-built for the community using them.

Our flagship product, Card Valuer, is an iOS app designed from the ground up for Pokemon TCG collectors and dealers — combining on-device machine learning with live market data to deliver instant, accurate card valuations from a phone camera.

HiWorld LLC is incorporated in the United States and operates as a software development and publishing company. We design, build, and maintain our own apps.
