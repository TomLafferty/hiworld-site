Privacy Policy
Card Valuer  ·  Last updated: April 27, 2026  ·  Developer: HiWorld LLC

Overview

Card Valuer is a Pokemon TCG card scanning and pricing app for iOS. This policy explains what data we access, how it is used, and what we do not do with it.

Data We Access

Camera
Card Valuer requires camera access to scan Pokemon TCG cards and graded slab barcodes. Camera frames are processed entirely on your device using Apple Vision (on-device OCR). No camera images or video are transmitted to any server.

Local Storage
Scan sessions, card lists, and collection data are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it via CSV.

Third-Party API Calls

When a card is identified via OCR, the app sends the card name and set number (text only — no images) to the PokeTrace API to retrieve market pricing data. No personal information is included in these requests.

ServiceData SentPurpose
PokeTrace (poketrace.com)Card name, set numberReal-time market pricing

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not store or transmit camera images
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use third-party analytics (no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude)

Data Retention

All app data (sessions, card lists, collection history) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all data permanently. We have no copy of your data.

Children's Privacy

Card Valuer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no account creation, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.
